Embedded Software Development

We specialize in embedded software for microcontrollers and custom hardware. Our team delivers robust, efficient firmware in the latest C standards, enabling reliable control, connectivity, and performance for your products. From device drivers to real-time applications, we turn complex requirements into working solutions.

Mechanical Design

We offer comprehensive mechanical design services, including expertise in CNC machined parts, sheet metal fabrication, and 3D printing. Our team can help take your concepts through to fully realized parts, providing high quality mechanical drawings, CFD, FEA, and tolerance analysis.

With experience in both analog and digital design, we ensure reliable, manufacturable solutions for prototypes and commercial products alike.
